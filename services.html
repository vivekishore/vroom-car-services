BATTERY

Avoid unexpected breakdowns and unwanted repair bills by checking the health and condition of your car's battery. Batteries are more susceptible to failure, so a few simple checks can help make sure it's performing at its best.

BRAKES

Out of the many parts in a brake system, the disc/rotor and pads could be considered the most important. The rotor is attached to the hub of the axle and the pads are mounted on the rotors. For efficient braking, you have to make sure both these components are in their best working condition.

OIL FILTER

Engine oil loses its effectiveness over time and needs to be replaced to extend the life of your car's engine. It is also essential to replace the oil filter every time your car's engine oil is replaced as this aids the oil being contamination and sludge free. Hence, oil change and oil filter replacement go hand in hand!
